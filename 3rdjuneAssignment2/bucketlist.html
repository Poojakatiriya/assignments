My Travel Bucket List

For me, travel isn't about crowded tourist spots — it's about connection, culture, and quiet beauty. Here's my list of dream destinations that nourish the soul.

1. Bali, Indonesia - The Island of Gods

From healing temples to jungle waterfalls, Bali is not just a place — it's a journey into balance, nature, and spiritual calm.

Visit Bali

2. Chefchaouen, Morocco - The Blue Pearl

A quiet town painted entirely in blue, Chefchaouen offers calm, color, and charm tucked away in Morocco's Rif Mountains.

Visit Chefchaouen

3. Northern Lights - Chasing Aurora in Norway

I dream of lying on snow under a glowing sky, watching ribbons of green and purple dance silently above. The Northern Lights are nature’s own fairytale show.

Chase the Lights in Norway
