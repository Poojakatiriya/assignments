Biography Of MilKha Singh

Early Life

Milkha Singh, also known as the Flying Sikh, was born in 1929 in Gobindpura, Punjab (now in Pakistan). He faced a difficult childhood during the Partition of India, but his determination led him to greatness.

Achivements

Milkha Singh was one of India's greatest athletes. He won multiple gold medals in the Asian Games and represented India in several Olympic Games.
His most memorable race was the 400m final in the 1960 Rome Olympics, where he narrowly missed a medal.

Legacy

Milkha Singh inspired generations of Indian athletes. His life story was later portrayed in the Bollywood movie "Bhaag Milkha Bhaag . He remains a symbol of resilience, discipline, and patriotism.
